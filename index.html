🎒 Before you go

Know before you go

The practical bits so everyone arrives happy and prepared.

🧳 What to pack

  • Reef-safe sunscreen & a hat
  • Bug spray (the jungle bites back)
  • Swimsuits & quick-dry clothes
  • Light rain jacket — green season is real
  • Sandals + one pair of sneakers
  • Refillable water bottle

🛂 Getting in

  • Passport valid 6+ months
  • Proof of onward travel
  • No visa needed for most short stays
  • Fly into SJO or LIR

💵 Good to know

  • Currency: Costa Rican colón (₡)
  • USD widely accepted; bring small bills
  • Language: Spanish — "Pura Vida!"
  • Tap water is generally safe

🌦️ Weather

  • Warm & tropical, 75–90°F
  • Dry season: Dec–Apr
  • Green season: May–Nov (afternoon rain)
  • Strong sun year-round — hydrate!
